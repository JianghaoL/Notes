## 2026.1.21 - The Juice is Loose

***Juice Classification***

*Active Juice:*
A movement, action, or visual that communicates a specific systemic intent that alerts the player to an upcoming interaction, mechanic, or choice that has direct design impact on gameplay.

*Passive Juice:*
A movement or visual that only contributes to an emotional feeling that does not have any gameplay consequence.

***The Process of Game Interaction***

**Output** - What the game tells the player.
**Perception** - The information the player understands from the game,
**Conginition** - Based on the information givem, what the player understands about the situation.
**Action** - What the player attempts to do based on the situation.
**Input** - How the player enters in the information into the game.
**Computation** - The computer processes the player input and represents it in game.
